Rules

To ensure a safe and friendly community, please follow these rules.

CHATTING RULES

General Conduct

  • 1.

    No Swearing/Bad Language:

    Never swear, use slurs, be toxic, or say anything racist. Respect everyone in the server.

  • 2.

    Be Mature & Respectful:

    No drama, no toxic replies, no picking fights. This is a chill place.

  • 3.

    Respect Other Cultures:

    Don’t mock, joke about, or disrespect anyone’s country, accent, religion, or background.

Server Interaction

  • 1.

    No Pinging Content Creators:

    If you ping any content creator (like @ItsMarwan) without reason, you'll get muted for 3 days.

  • 2.

    No Self-Promotions:

    Only send your maps in the #your-builds channel. If you're caught promoting, you'll get muted for 5 days.

  • 3.

    Helping Others:

    Don’t say “idk” or “help me pls” without trying. If you can’t help, stay quiet. Breaking this rule results in a 1-week mute.

  • 4.

    No Mini-Modding:

    Do not act like a mod unless you are one. Let the staff do their job.

KEEPING THE SERVER SAFE

  • 1.

    Dealing with Bugs:

    Found a bug or server issue? DM @ItsMarwan with details so it can be fixed.

  • 2.

    Reporting Users:

    Tag @report_user and explain the issue clearly. Misusing this gets you a 2-day mute.

  • 3.

    Discord TOS:

    Always follow Discord’s Terms of Service. Breaking them can get you banned from this server & Discord itself.

  • 4.

    Suspicious Activity:

    Don’t share scam links, phishing, or fake giveaways. Instant ban if caught.

  • 5.

    Hacked Accounts:

    If your account was hacked or acts suspiciously, you will be temporarily banned until verified.

  • 6.

    Privacy:

    Don’t share private DMs, personal info, or other people’s content without permission.

EVENT RULES

  • 1.

    No Cheating:

    If you’re caught cheating or breaking event rules, you’ll be disqualified and possibly banned.

  • 2.

    Respect Event Hosts:

    If you're participating in a tournament, contest, or giveaway, follow the host’s instructions.

  • 3.

    Don’t Ask for Wins:

    Asking for free wins, roles, or giveaway rerolls will get you disqualified.

  • 4.

    One Account Only:

    Do not join events using alts. Only your main account is allowed.

MORE CHATTING RULES

Moderation and Pings

  • 1.

    Listen to Moderators & @ItsMarwan:

    Mods are here to help. Respect their decisions or you might get muted.

  • 2.

    No Spamming @Mods or Help:

    Spamming help requests or pinging mods without reason will result in a mute and no support.

Prohibited Topics & Behaviors

  • 1.

    No Politics or Religion:

    These topics aren’t allowed. If you start such discussions, you’ll be banned for 3 weeks.

  • 2.

    No Begging:

    Don’t beg for roles, ranks, free stuff, or special permissions.

CHANNEL USAGE

  • 1.

    Support Channels:

    Only ask for help in #community or #ask-marwan. Not in #general or off-topic channels.

  • 2.

    Channel Usage:

    Use each channel for its intended purpose. Read the description before typing.

  • 3.

    Usage of #ask-marwan:

    This is only for direct questions to @ItsMarwan. Don’t use #general to ask him anything.

  • 4.

    Follow Forums Rules:

    Some forums have rules or templates. Read them before posting.

  • 5.

    Media Channels:

    You can send images, but no NSFW or inappropriate content. Video ideas must follow Epic Games' Terms of Service.

  • 6.

    English Only:

    This server uses English. You can chat in other languages in designated channels if available.

CHANNEL RESTRICTIONS

  • 1.

    Links:

    Only allowed in #your-builds and #promotions. Posting links elsewhere = warning + link deleted.

  • 2.

    Spam Messages:

    No spamming texts, emojis, images, or repeated messages.

  • 3.

    Server Invites:

    Don’t send invites to other servers unless it’s in #promotions.

  • 4.

    NSFW/Offensive Content:

    This is a family-friendly server. No NSFW content anywhere. Doing so results in an instant ban.

  • 5.

    Impersonation:

    Don’t pretend to be staff, creators, or other members. This gets you banned.

  • 6.

    AI & Bot Use:

    Only use bots in the bot channels. Don’t spam bot commands in general chat.

  • 7.

    Promotions & Job Board Rules:

    You cannot sell or promote anything against the Epic Games Terms of Service in the #promotions or #job-board channels.

  • 8.

    Asset Trading Rules & Disclaimer:

    Warning: Trades and assets shared here are not affiliated with the server. Verify everything before downloading or trading.

    • We do not own, sell, or distribute any of the assets posted in this forum.
    • If you're the rightful owner of any asset and want it removed, please contact the moderators.
    You trade, download, or share content at your own risk.
